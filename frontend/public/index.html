Formanti — Your AI Sports Coach

Formanti is an AI-powered multi-sport coaching platform. Upload a match or practice video to get instant, frame-by-frame technique analysis, then follow personalized training plans, get smart equipment recommendations, take skill assessments and track your progress over time.

Sports we coach

  • Badminton — smash speed, footwork and serve analysis
  • Tennis — serve, forehand and backhand technique
  • Table Tennis — serve, spin and stroke technique
  • Pickleball — technique and strategy for beginners
  • Cricket — batting and bowling technique analysis
  • Football — dribbling, shooting and ball control
  • Swimming — freestyle and stroke technique
  • Basketball — shooting form, dribbling and vertical

What you get
